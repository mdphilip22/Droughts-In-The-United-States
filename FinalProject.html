Drought Conditions in the Continental United States

With conversations about climate and weather happenning every day, does the US have more droughts than it used to?

December 2nd 2022, By Michael Philipkosky

In recent times, there have been more and more attention paid to climate change around the world. The most recent climate summit in Glasgow Scotland in 2021 had over 200 countries that took part in creating the Glasgow Climate Pact. The main goal of this summit was to keep global temperatures from rising more than 1.5 degrees. Additionally it set expectations and goals for the world in the coming years. Climate change causes and enhances many different weather events from floods, hurricanes and droughts. A drought is when an area experiences a prolonged period of dryness.
